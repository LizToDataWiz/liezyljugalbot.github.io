About Me

Hey there! I'm Liezyl Jugalbot and I was born and raised in the Philippines. I've worked in banking for over a decade and now I'm in Canada trying to transition my career into data analysis. I'm excited to use my analytical skills to extract insights and help solve business challenges.

I'm currently on the hunt for a Data Analyst role, and I'm really interested in companies that value work-life balance. I'm a pretty adaptable and positive person, and I'm always looking to grow and improve. I'm excited about the chance to make a positive impact at a company!

Albert Einstein's wisdom has always resonated with me, especially his belief that we should never stop growing intellectually. As a new Data Analyst, I'm excited to adopt this mindset and see each challenge as an opportunity to expand my knowledge and make a meaningful contribution to the field. I'm dedicated to delivering excellence in all aspects of my work and I'm looking forward to working collaboratively with everyone involved to achieve our shared goals.

Together, let's learn and discover continuously to make a real impact on the world.
